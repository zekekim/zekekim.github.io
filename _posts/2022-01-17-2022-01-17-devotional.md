Wow. What an amazing passage! I was really tired these past few weeks, and along with this passage, I remember how weak and utterly unsustainable I am, but as it says that those who find hope in the Lord will renew their strength, I am so happy to hear this! God gives me comfort not in knowing that I will be okay, but knowing that I can find hope and peace in God and himself.

To rely on God is something that I am working on daily and probably something I will be working on for the rest of my life. I want to be able to completely give my will to him, and work in a way that is pleasing to him. May God be my strength and my rest, my time to cry and time to rejoice. Let all things come from God and me in Him.

**Prayer**

Dear God, thank you for a new day. I'm really excited to start today and be energized off of my 10 hours of sleep I got last night. I pray that you will be able to make this a daily routine in my life! God please help me to reach out to you in my times of struggling, and not only reaching out, but continually seeking you for guidance in my life. Even more so, I want you to control me and use me. Let it be known to me that I am nothing without you, and apart from you I can do nothing. I am tired of trying to do things by myself, and ultimately being let down by the world. It is to no avail and no pure satisfaction. Please continue to help me rely and seek you, that is my forever prayer.
