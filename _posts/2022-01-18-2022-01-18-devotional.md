Honestly as much as I read this verse, I feel pretty neutral inside. I know that this is one of the most crucial things to believe as a Christian, but I don't feel that way for some reason. I pray that the resurrection would really resound with me, and become something new to me. Why is Jesus' resurrection such a big deal?

**Prayer**

Dear God, please help me to really reflect on your death and resurrection, and why it is important to me as a Christian. I want you to show me why it's so important, and soften my heart to the truth. God, let me understand you and seek you everyday, and let me lean not on my own understandings but everything that you provide. Amen.
