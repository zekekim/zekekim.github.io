What an interesting couple of months. I haven't been posting on here, and mostly it's because im lazy, but also because so much has happened. This year, My goal is to pursue a full and individual relationship with God. That's not something that I thought I would be doing even a few months before, but I am so happy right now. I am more than happy, I am overflowing with joy.

![mingki and ezra lol](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/mh7sXyMsEv0FdJ4ukccV5N2W7W25gTnWOusZOwbIEWptyDNBg7hbIZVh9mYMZWf6NFzI5MUP48etwqQ1W9rkvgrpHiYFwC9qVOLCiTdUoEnohtsz91Pe6oAvbm1I-YCI-lUnqW7E74A=w2400)

I think the thing that made it hard for me to initially enjoy having a relationship with God, was the fact that it was not enjoyable. I thought that a relationship with God meant no pleasure in God, that God was your pleasure and that it was below whatever the world had to offer me. But God wants us to enjoy his presence and find happiness in it! And ultimately, God is for me and I should be for God. He deserves everything in and of me, and I want to belong to him.

Things like providing snacks and food for us while we were sick with COVID, buying food for us, texting us to make sure we're okay, etc. They let me rely on them, and are happy that I do, and that is something that is truly amazing. It's really interesting to see myself grow, and it's such a radical change. I feel like I'm a new person, and I'm ecstatic that that is the case. Of course it is slow and sometimes really painful, but I am happy about it nonetheless.

There are still things that stress me out though, and here they are:
- Internships over the summer.
- Getting a job right now.
- Overcoming my fear of God's plan for me.

I like to be in control. I have really bad trust issues, and have never formed a true relationship with someone where I completely rely on them. I don't think I even shouldnt! But God is some thing that I can trust and that is something I am working on.

Besides that, I have had some run in with romance that I'm currently learning to kind of let go, because there's so much that I need to pursue first. I want to work on myself so I can be someone that is not necessarily worthy, but someone that can lean on God and can trust him fully.

I have gotten closer to my younger and older brother, and I want to be there for them and shower them with love. I have had a stronger urge to serve them and show God's love to them.

My dad has been someone I always feel free to disrespect and talk back to, and I am learning to bite my tongue and have more thoughts to my words. I want to honor my parents because they have done so much for me, and God has commanded it!
