Jesus delivers this cautionary message in order to warn the Pharisees of their hipocrisy and prevent others from seeing their ways as wise. 

>11 The greatest among you will be your servant.12 For those who exalt themselves will be humbled, and those who humble themselves will be exalted. Matthew 23:11-12

I think many times I am like a Pharisee. I'm someone who is a people pleaser, not all the time but a good amount of the time. I really enjoy when others pat me on the back, and maybe thats just natural, but often it can influence me to make the wrong decisions in order to look better in front of others. I want to be humbled and broken, from a wild horse to a domesticized horse.

**Prayer**

Dear God thank you for this day. Thank you for my vinyl records that bring me so much joy to play, and for the warm blanket that allows me to sleep comfortably at night. I pray that you will help me to humble myself, to remember why I am here and who you are that assures me of my inability and furthers my perception of your ability. I want to lean on you, and to let you take control of my life. I want you to be the driver of my life. Please take control, and help me to want to want you. This is going to be tough, but I know that you can do anything and apart from you I am nothing. Please let me realize this. Amen.
